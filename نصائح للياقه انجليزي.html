My Healthy Routine

Your guide to a balanced and healthy life every day

Healthy Routine Tips

  • Start with realistic and achievable goals.
  • Choose habits that fit your daily schedule.
  • Enhance your environment with healthy tools.
  • Seek support from friends or family.
  • Track your progress and reward yourself.

Prevent Injuries During Workouts

  • Begin with a 5-10 minute warm-up.
  • Practice exercises gradually.
  • Maintain correct body posture.
  • Rest when feeling overly tired.
  • Use suitable sports clothes and shoes.

Nutrition Tips

  • Eat vegetables and fruits daily.
  • Drink at least 8 cups of water.
  • Reduce sugar and fast food.
  • Have a protein-rich breakfast.
  • Balance carbohydrates and healthy fats.

The Importance of Good Sleep

  • Sleep 7 to 9 hours daily.
  • Keep regular sleep and wake times.
  • Avoid electronic devices one hour before bed.
  • Create a quiet and dark sleeping environment.
