Healthy Food

The Role of Nutrition in Fitness

Diet Plan for Muscle Gain

Breakfast

Includes eggs, whole grain bread, and Greek yogurt to support early muscle growth.

Lunch

Grilled chicken breast with brown rice and vegetables to provide protein and energy.

Dinner

Tuna with green salad and some nuts to help the body recover overnight.

Diet Plan for Weight Loss

Snack

An apple or cucumber with a cup of green tea to reduce hunger without many calories.

Healthy Lunch

Grilled chicken breast or fish with a fiber-rich green salad.

Light Dinner

Vegetable soup or low-fat yogurt to ease digestion before sleep.

Healthy Recipes

Quinoa Salad

Cooked quinoa with fresh vegetables, olive oil, and lemon juice.

Lentil Soup

Lentils, carrots, onions, and warm spices – rich in protein and fiber.

Energy Smoothie

Banana, spinach, apple, and almond milk – a natural and healthy energy drink.
