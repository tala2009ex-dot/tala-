Strength Exercises

Flexibility exercises improve the range of motion in your joints, reduce injury risk, and relieve muscle tension, enhancing physical performance and promoting relaxation.

  • Back Stretch
  • Leg Stretch

Cardio workouts improve heart health, boost fitness, and help burn fat, reducing the risk of chronic diseases and improving overall mood.
